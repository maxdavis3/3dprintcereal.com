Cereal & Snack Development

01

Custom Shape Prototyping

Rapid prototype batches of novel cereal shapes from CAD concepts. Test character features, brand logos, and nutritional perforations before committing to production tooling. Dough formulation consultation included.

02

Licensed Character Development

Character-accurate cereal shapes from approved 3D assets. Fine facial feature detail at cereal scale. Licensing and production pathway support for brand partnerships and seasonal product lines.

03

Nutritional Geometry

Surface-area-optimized shapes for faster milk absorption, reduced caloric density, or enhanced coating adhesion. Honeycomb, lattice, and perforated ring geometries tested at prototype scale before production die investment.

FORMULATION

Cereal Dough Compatibility

Our printing platform works with conventional cereal dough recipes — no proprietary ingredients required for shape prototyping.

  • ● Corn, wheat, rice, and oat base dough formulations
  • ● Whole grain and high-fiber dough compatibility
  • ● Honey, chocolate, and berry flavor dough variants
  • ● Gluten-free alternative grain formulations
  • ● Post-print sugar coating and frosting simulation
  • ● Batch reproducibility for production spec submission
